FAQs

Got Questions?

Everything you need to know before booking your first SOFACLEAN appointment.

How long does sofa cleaning take? +
A standard 3-seater sofa takes approximately 45–60 minutes to clean. Larger sectionals or full-home packages take 2–4 hours. We'll give you an accurate time estimate when you book.
How long does the sofa take to dry? +
Using our high-velocity drying equipment, most sofas are dry to the touch within 1–2 hours. We recommend waiting 2–3 hours before sitting on the sofa for best results.
Are your cleaning products safe for children and pets? +
Absolutely. All our cleaning solutions are non-toxic, biodegradable, and free from harsh chemicals. They are fully safe for babies, children, and pets. We use the same products in our own homes.
Can you remove old or set-in stains? +
In most cases, yes. Our pre-treatment solutions and high-pressure steam can remove stains that have set in for months or even years. Results depend on the stain type and fabric — we'll always give you an honest assessment.
Do I need to move my furniture? +
No. Our technicians handle everything. Just ensure there's a clear path to the sofa and access to a power socket. We bring all equipment with us.
What areas do you serve? +
We currently operate in Karachi, Lahore, and Islamabad, covering all major residential areas. If you're unsure about your location, just give us a call and we'll confirm availability.
